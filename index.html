Inequality: pay gap

We decided to choose a topic that affects people in both countries. As students that are soon to be graduates we decided to focus on workspace inequality. Specifically we are going to be talking about the pay gap between men and women.

Why is it important to talk about this issue?

Despite numbers showing that the gender pay gap is still a reality to this day, many misconceptions persist. The only way to ensure people are paid based on their qualifications and job description rather than something they cannot change such as their gender or race is by combating ignorance. Therefore, it is important to open the conversation so that we can dispel myths and face the problem head on.

How will we start the conversation?

We want to raise awareness about this inequality through the videos we made, you can find them below. The first video is about a group of friends who get together in a video call to catch up. But when they start talking about their jobs they quickly realize that there is a big difference between how much money the men and the women earn. It's a more playful video, but don't be fooled by that because it has a powerful message. The second video is a short documentary which shows that the wage gap is a global problem. We did this by compiling 4 interviews with people from both Belgium and Mexico.
